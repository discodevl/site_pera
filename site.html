i felt the sea talking to me one day, on a acid trip. it told me things i've yet to understand, but i feel it was all about allowing myself to go on new journeys. in search of a place i saw when looking at the sea, a place i know exists somewhere inside me. the word "scuopnuo" appeared to me and another friend during that experience. searching for it online i couldn't find anything, which leads me to believe that we created it, on a trip, at the same time. that was a cool experience, and i wanted it to be the starting point of something. welcome to my personal growth journey through the world of disruptive a/v art.
